About Cumilla Polytechnic Institute High Tech Club

The Cumilla Polytechnic Institute High Tech Club (CPI HTC) is a student-led community focused on practical learning, technology projects, and peer mentorship. We organize workshops, coding sessions, project showcases, and collaborative initiatives that help members build real-world skills.

Vision

To create an inclusive, hands-on learning ecosystem that prepares students for technology careers and empowers them to contribute to local and global tech communities.

Mission

  • Provide free or low-cost hands-on workshops and training.
  • Support student projects from idea to deployment.
  • Host contests and study groups to improve problem-solving skills.
  • Build partnerships that create internship and career opportunities.

What we do

Regular meetups, domain-focused workshops (web, mobile, AI/ML, networking), collaborative projects, hackathons, career talks, and peer mentorship. Members can propose events and lead initiatives.

How to join

Visit our Join Us page to sign up. Membership is open to all CPI students; we welcome contributors from every department.
