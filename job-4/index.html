Welcome to Cumilla Polytechnic Institute

The Cumilla Polytechnic Institute High Tech Club (CPI HTC) is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering innovation, learning, and collaboration in technology. Formerly known as the CPI Programming Club, CPI HTC has grown its focus to cover a broad range of high-tech fields and practical skills.

Key activities and focus areas

CPI HTC's mission is to empower students with skills and resources for success in the tech industry. The club runs regular activities across several areas:

  • Workshops & training: Free, hands-on workshops (for example, beginner Python sessions and a "Zero to Professional" web development course).
  • Competitive programming: Contests and practice sessions to build problem-solving skills in data structures and algorithms.
  • Modern technologies: Exploration and projects in AI/ML, full-stack web & app development, networking, graphics design, and cybersecurity.
  • Career guidance: Events such as career summits and partnership-driven "Tech Career Hunts" providing industry insights and mentorship.
  • Resource sharing & projects: A hub for collaborative projects, resource exchange, and building real-world applications.
  • Leadership development: Activities that build teamwork, presentation skills, and leadership experience.

Who can join

CPI HTC primarily serves students of Cumilla Polytechnic Institute and welcomes members from multiple departments, including Computer Science & Technology, Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electronics & Telecommunication.

If you'd like to get involved, attend a club workshop or follow CPI HTC's announcements for upcoming events and projects. The club provides a practical, hands-on path for students to grow technical and professional skills while contributing to a collaborative community.
